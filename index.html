Features

Everything Your
Business Needs

One platform. Infinite conversations. From first contact to loyal customer — ClawBot handles it all without breaking a sweat.

Natural Language Understanding

ClawBot goes beyond keyword matching. It understands intent, context, sentiment, and nuance — just like a human agent but 10x faster and always available. Powered by next-gen transformer architecture with 175B+ parameter comprehension.

Multi-Platform Integration

Deploy ClawBot on your website, WhatsApp, Telegram, Slack, Instagram, and more — all from a single dashboard. REST API, native plugins, and webhooks make integration seamless in under 10 minutes.

24/7 Autonomous Support

Never miss a message. ClawBot operates around the clock — holidays, weekends, 3 AM — with zero downtime. Smart escalation routes complex queries to human agents when confidence thresholds aren't met.

Smart Analytics Dashboard

Track every conversation metric that matters. Sentiment trends, resolution rates, drop-off points, A/B test results, and CSAT scores — all visualized in real-time. Export full conversation history anytime.

Custom Personality & Tone

Train ClawBot on your brand voice, FAQs, product docs, and PDFs. Set personality traits — professional, friendly, witty — so every response feels uniquely yours. White-label for agencies included in Enterprise.

Enterprise-Grade Security

End-to-end encryption, role-based access control, GDPR compliance, and SOC 2 certification. Your data never trains public models. Built-in abuse filtering and spam detection keep every conversation safe.
